Ansys Explicit Dynamics

Understanding material behavior at high strain rates is important in the defense industry, here is an example of a solid copper projectile impacting an aluminum plate. Due to strain rates and time scales, explicit dynamic analysis is necessary.

The red dots in this video show material failure; and so throughout the sequence, failed elements become red nodes. These two sequences show material strain and stress in the z direction. The intent was to show stress wave propogation because in high-G environments this is often where components fail, not due to the peak-g but the cyclical stress waves afterwards. If you look closely, you can see the compressive and tensile stress waves in the second sequence.
